Networking

▶ Understanding the Company Network
Learn about LAN, Wi-Fi, VPN, and how to connect securely.

The company network consists of both wired (LAN) and wireless (Wi-Fi) connections. VPN (Virtual Private Network) allows secure remote access. Always use strong passwords and never share your credentials. For more details, refer to the IT handbook or contact your supervisor.

▶ Troubleshooting Network Issues
Basic steps to diagnose and resolve common network problems.

If you experience connectivity issues, first check your cables and Wi-Fi connection. Restart your device if needed. If the problem persists, contact IT support and provide details about the issue, including error messages and steps already taken.
